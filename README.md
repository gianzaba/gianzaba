### 🚀 Featured Project: TicketApp

> Ticket sales platform connecting event organizers with their audience. The system manages three key roles:

👤 Users: Explore events, select seats in real-time, purchase tickets, and receive their QR tickets as PDFs.

🏢 Organizers: Create and manage their own events and view sales statistics.

🛡️ Admins: Moderate the platform by approving or rejecting events and managing users.

This project was about much more than just learning programming languages; it was an immersive experience in modern software engineering. We focused on using best practices and agile methodologies to enhance our teamwork.
